Children's Museum

Ceili Kisvarday (photo bombed by her little brother, Zoli), Feb 5, 2017
Our little town opened a children's museum (pre-pandemic) which my husband helped design. This is a picture of Ceili participating in a fund-raiser for the museum; children get to paint their handprints on the wall; the whole family still has handprints on the wall today. This picture happened to be taken on her birthday.
